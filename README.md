## Middle Kingdom Aerospace

Middle Kingdom Aerospace (MKA) is a new mod made by Kari and Questin focused on the 长征火箭 (Chang Zheng/Long March) rocket family (maybe more in the future?). Our goal is to bring a high quality version of the CZ family to KSP. While on SEP we took some liberties due to the multiple changes SpaceX makes between prototypes, MKA aims to recreate as close as possible every single operational rocket from the family.
Development has already started with CZ-5 and will focus on newer generation first.
